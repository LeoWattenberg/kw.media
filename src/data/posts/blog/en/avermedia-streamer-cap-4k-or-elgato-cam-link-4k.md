AVerMedia has released a new capture card, and they sent me a sample to take a closer look at and share my thoughts. I made sure to approach the comparison as objectively as possible.

The [AVerMedia Streamer Cap4K (BU113)](https://www.avermedia.com/de/product-detail/bu113?utm_source=PR&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=BU113_NPI) is designed to connect DSLR cameras to a PC for recording or streaming using recording software such as OBS or AVerMedia's own RECentral. This makes it a direct competitor to Elgato's Camlink4K. So, our central question today is: Can AVerMedia overcome Elgato's market dominance and convince with cheaper products?

<figure class="image-full"><img width="1000" height="400" src="/assets/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/camlink_cap4k_vergleich.jpg" alt></figure>

First and foremost, both capture devices do exactly what they're supposed to. The image is crystal clear on both, and even during long-term testing—in our case, 3-4 hour livestreams—I didn't notice any delays or image errors.

## Dimensions, Setup & Heat Management:

When considering these devices, we must look at their size and, consequently, their passive heat capacity. While the Camlink4K is slightly smaller at 9.42 x 4.54 x 1.60 cm, it must be connected to the motherboard I/O and its awkward shape blocks two additional USB ports on my backpanel, including USB 3.0 ports. The [Cap4K](https://www.avermedia.com/de/product-detail/bu113?utm_source=PR&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=BU113_NPI) is larger at 9.42 x 4.54 x 1.60 cm but connects via a USB 3.0 cable, allowing it to be placed on the desk closer to the camera. AVerMedia definitely has an advantage here as the [Cap4K](https://www.avermedia.com/de/product-detail/bu113?utm_source=PR&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=BU113_NPI) doesn't get warm at all. Both devices were equally easy to set up as video capture devices in OBS and could be manipulated well in ChromaKey mode and scaling.

## Availability:

Both the [Cap4K](https://www.avermedia.com/de/product-detail/bu113?utm_source=PR&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=BU113_NPI) and the Camlink are available for Amazon Same-Day-Delivery. So, if the device were to fail after several thousand operating hours, you could quickly get a replacement.

## Conclusion:

Is the [AVerMedia Cap4K (BU113)](https://www.avermedia.com/de/product-detail/bu113?utm_source=PR&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=BU113_NPI) superior to the Elgato Camlink4K? Qualitatively, I see no differences—you simply save €30 with AVerMedia. So, yes, in terms of price-performance ratio, the [Cap4K (BU113)](https://www.avermedia.com/de/product-detail/bu113?utm_source=PR&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=BU113_NPI) is superior.
