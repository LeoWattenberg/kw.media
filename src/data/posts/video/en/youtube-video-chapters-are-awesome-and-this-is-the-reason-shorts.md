## Transcript

If you're watching a video with chapters, on desktop you can click on the current chapter below the video. This opens a list of all chapters on the right, allowing you to quickly jump to other chapters. It's even easier in the YouTube app: you can double-tap with two fingers on the left or right side of the video. This will take you back or forward one chapter.
