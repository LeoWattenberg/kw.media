Does your name in live chat look like a barcode or bot? YouTube just updated the chat logic. Display names are gone. You're now only your [ad handle](/youtube-tips-en/what-are-youtube-handles-aliases/). Don't want to be at user 123? Fix it right now. Go to studio.YouTube.com. Letters, numbers, underscores, and dots are fine, but no URLs, and no phone numbers. And be careful. You can only change this twice every 14 days.
