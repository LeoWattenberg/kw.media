## Transcript

YouTube ads can be displayed higher up in search. In some cases it's possible to bid on keywords. The price is defined by the bidding strategy and can increase the click-through rate on your ads and drive more potential viewers to your channel. To find suitable keywords, use the Google Ads Keyword Planner. You can get an overview of the different prices and how often they are searched for. Using the right keywords is a good option to make it easy for the right audience to find you.
