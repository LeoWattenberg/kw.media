We also have some license issues regarding YouTube Shorts and creator music:

- **Vixen Music:** This music label has retracted their Shorts audio library licenses from March 28th, 2025. If you've used Vixen music in your shorts, you'll receive an email about it, and those shorts will be removed. Long-form videos with Vixen content will remain but may receive copyright claims without additional agreements.

- **Warner Music Group (WMG):** Licenses for WMG songs obtained via YouTube's Creator Music are being revoked and turned into copyright claims, with all proceeds going to WMG. If you've used YouTube Music or Creator Music, check your emails and ensure you have licenses from elsewhere. Always have your paperwork in order to avoid legal issues.

While not groundbreaking, these tools could be useful for generating viewer-suggested content and smoother video introductions.
