## Transcript

To search for a hashtag on YouTube, go to the search bar and enter the desired hashtag. Directly below the search bar, you will now see the hashtag channel, which you can access by simply clicking on it. You can also access the hashtag overview via videos. To do this, just go to the video description and click on the desired hashtag. For more information about YouTube, subscribe to the kw.media channel.
